Selling a watch

Learn what you can do to create more attractive listings and improve your sales chances.

TIP 1. Use High-Quality Images


Take high-quality pictures of your watch from every angle.


The number of images you upload also has a major impact on the attractiveness of your listing. Photos are the best way for users to get a better sense of the watch's condition. Ideally, you should upload at least 8 images.

TIP 2. Set the right price


Setting a realistic price increases your sales chances. Make sure it's a sum you'd be willing to accept without going overboard. Don't forget any accessories or if you have the original box and papers! This increases your watch's value.
